CroAppella is a Croatian a cappella group founded in 2015.
They perform modern pop songs using only vocal arrangements.

Their founder, Ivan Horvat, is also the main arranger and beatboxer.

The group won the 2019 National A Cappella Festival.

They perform mostly in Croatian, but sometimes do English covers too.

CroAppella has 6 members: 2 sopranos, 1 alto, 1 tenor, 1 baritone, and 1 bass.
